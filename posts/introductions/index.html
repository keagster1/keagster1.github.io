Introduction Link to heading

Hello and welcome to my blog!

For my blog, I will be writing about my journey both as a Software Engineer but also as a person. I hope you enjoy the content I put out and I hope you learn something from it!

More About Me Link to heading

I was born in the 90’s, a time when computers were just beginning to enter the mainstream. My early years are filled with memories of the iconic dial-up modem sounds, VCR tapes, and the nostalgia-inducing floppy disks. I still get a wave of nostalgia thinking about those days, especially when I remember the hours spent on Windows XP and Windows 7.

But technology wasn’t always my passion. In fact, it wasn’t until a particular moment — a game-changing (literally!) day when my mom bought me an Xbox 360 Arcade — that I really began to see the magic in technology. That moment sparked a curiosity that would lead me down the path to becoming a Software Engineer.

Looking Forward Link to heading

This blog isn’t just about my past; it’s about the ongoing journey. I plan to write about everything from coding challenges and new technologies to personal growth and life lessons. Whether you’re here for the tech insights or the personal stories, I hope you find something valuable in my posts.
